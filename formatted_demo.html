Cosmetic Products: Global Definition

The definition of "cosmetic" products varies across countries but generally refers to items used for cleansing, beautification, or enhancing appearance on the human body.

This definition is influenced by both internal factors like a person's age, ethnicity, and health condition, as well as external factors such as environmental conditions like dryness, temperature, and wind.

💡 Insights

  • Understanding the diverse definitions of 'cosmetic' products across cultures can be valuable for product development and marketing
  • Cultural beauty standards significantly impact consumer preferences
